Pad Thai Ingredients: 2 eggs, 300g of rice noodles, 10 prawns, 2 tbs of dried turnip, 2 handfuls of bean sprouts, Chinese chives chopped, Fried tofu, cut into small pieces. For the sauce: 3 tbs of tamarin paste, 1 tbs vegetable oil, 3 shallots chopped, 2 tbs of palm sugar, 2 tbs Thai fish sauce and 1 tps dark soy sauce
Thai green curry Ingredients: 1 tbs of vegetable oil, 3 tbs of green curry paste, 400ml of coconut milk, 1 tbs of palm sugar, 2 tbs of Thai fish sauce, pinch of seasalt, 3 lime leaves, 500g boneless chicken breast, 100g pea aubergines, Thai aubergines, 150g cooked bamboo shoots and a handful of basil leaves.
Pu Nim Ingredients: 4 soft-shell crabs, 6 coriander roots, salt, 8-10 garlic cloves, 1 tsp of black peppercorns, 3-4 tbs of all-purpose flour, vegetable oil and 1 tbs of chopped coriander leaves.
